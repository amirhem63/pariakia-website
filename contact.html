FAQ

Common questions.

How do you work with clients?

I work remotely with clients worldwide, with a preference for weekly or bi-weekly video calls and close collaboration via Figma and Notion. For UK clients, I'm available for in-person workshops in London.

What does a typical project look like?

Projects usually start with a discovery phase to understand your users and goals, followed by structured design sprints with regular review checkpoints. Most projects run for 6–16 weeks depending on scope.

Do you work with startups?

Yes — I enjoy working with ambitious startups alongside established brands. If you're an early-stage company, let's talk about what makes sense for your stage and budget.

What's the minimum project size?

I typically work on projects starting at £5,000. For longer engagements or fractional UX Strategist roles, monthly retainers are available.

How quickly can you start?

Current availability varies. Reach out and I'll let you know. For urgent projects, I may be able to accommodate a faster start depending on scope.

Do you do development too?

My focus is product design and UX — not development. However, I provide thorough developer handoff documentation and am happy to support your development team throughout the build phase.
