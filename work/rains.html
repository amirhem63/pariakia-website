Rains — Shopify
E-commerce Experience

Elevating the online shopping experience for a premium Danish outerwear brand through clarity, calm design, and a conversion-focused approach.

01 — Challenge

A premium brand with a digital experience that didn't match.

Rains is a Copenhagen-based outerwear brand known for its premium quality, minimalist aesthetic, and loyal following. Their Shopify store had grown organically alongside the brand, but the digital experience had fallen behind — cluttered product pages, an unclear navigation structure, and a checkout flow that was losing customers at every step.

The brief was clear: redesign the Shopify experience to match the premium positioning of the brand and convert significantly more of the growing traffic the brand was attracting through its marketing investments.

02 — Discovery

Research that uncovered the real friction points.

Before designing a single pixel, I conducted a thorough discovery phase: analysing Hotjar heatmaps and session recordings, reviewing Google Analytics funnels, and conducting 8 user interviews with Rains customers across different purchase stages.

The research revealed three critical issues: users were struggling to find the right product due to poor filtering and category organisation; the product pages lacked the contextual information needed to justify the premium price point; and the checkout was losing customers at the shipping step due to a lack of transparency.

◎

Navigation confusion

67% of sessions involved users navigating back to the homepage — a sign of disorientation in the category structure.

◈

Price justification gap

User interviews revealed that shoppers needed more material, craft, and quality information before committing to a £200+ purchase.

◻

Shipping anxiety

Checkout drop-off was 3× higher at the shipping step than industry average, driven by late disclosure of delivery costs.

◇

Mobile underperformance

Mobile accounted for 71% of traffic but only 38% of revenue — a significant conversion gap to close.

03 — Design

Clarity, calm, and confidence at every step.

The redesign started from a simple principle: every screen should do one job exceptionally well, and remove everything that doesn't serve it.

For the homepage, I stripped back the visual clutter and introduced a editorial-style layout that led with seasonal storytelling before transitioning cleanly into product discovery. The navigation was restructured around customer mindsets rather than internal product hierarchies.

Product pages were redesigned to front-load the material and sustainability story that Rains customers care about, with a sticky add-to-cart that remained visible without competing with the content above it. Mobile received particular attention — a completely reimagined swipe-friendly image gallery, and a size guide that opened inline rather than breaking the shopping flow.

04 — Results

Measurable impact, immediately visible.

Within 8 weeks of launch, the results confirmed what the research had predicted. The changes to product discovery and navigation reduced bounce rate by 18% and increased pages-per-session by 31%. The updated product pages drove a 44% increase in average time on site.

Most significantly, the combined effect of clearer navigation, richer product pages, and a transparent checkout flow delivered a 32% increase in overall conversion rate — and a 28% reduction in basket abandonment.

"Paria understood our brand immediately. The new experience feels completely in line with who we are — premium, considered, and effortless. The commercial results have been exceptional."
— E-commerce Director, Rains
